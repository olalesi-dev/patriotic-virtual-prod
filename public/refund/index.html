Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: March 18, 2026 | Last Updated: March 18, 2026

1. Consultation Fees

Consultation fees cover the cost of a clinical evaluation by a licensed provider. If a provider determines that treatment is not clinically appropriate for you, you will receive a full refund of the consultation fee within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

2. Subscription Plans

If you enrolled in a monthly or ongoing care plan:

  • Cancellation before next billing cycle: Cancel at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting support. No further charges will be made after the current billing period ends.
  • Cancellation after billing: Charges for the current billing period are non-refundable. You retain access to services through the end of the paid period.
  • Annual plans: Pro-rated refunds are available within 30 days of annual plan purchase. After 30 days, no refunds are issued for annual plans.

3. Prescription & Medication Orders

Once a prescription has been transmitted to a pharmacy and the order has been processed or shipped, refunds for medication costs are subject to the pharmacy's independent refund policy. Patriotic Telehealth does not control pharmacy billing or dispensing.

If a medication is returned due to a fulfillment error, we will work with our pharmacy partner to arrange a replacement or refund.

4. Non-Refundable Items & Telehealth Finality

The following are strictly non-refundable:

  • Completed Consultations: Once a telehealth consultation is performed by a licensed provider, no refunds can be issued under any circumstances, regardless of the treatment outcome or prescription eligibility.
  • Prescriptions: Once a prescription has been sent to the pharmacy, the cost of the medical consult and the medication are final and non-refundable under any circumstances.
  • Lab order fees after specimen collection.
  • Subscription fees for periods already accessed.

5. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, contact our support team within 14 days of the charge:

  • Email: hello@patriotictelehealth.com
  • Phone: (202) 215-0636
  • Address: 176 NW 25th St, Miami, FL 33127

Include your name, account email, transaction date, and reason for the refund request. We will respond within 3 business days.

6. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback with your financial institution without first contacting us, we reserve the right to suspend your account pending investigation. We encourage you to contact us directly to resolve any billing concerns.

7. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Medical Disclaimer: This platform provides telehealth services. A licensed provider will evaluate each patient to determine clinical appropriateness. No treatment is guaranteed. Individual results vary.
